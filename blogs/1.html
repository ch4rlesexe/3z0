The Evolution of Web Development

Published on January 14th, 2025 at 4:35pm by Charlie.

Web development has undergone a massive transformation over the years. From simple text-based websites to dynamic applications powered by artificial intelligence, the journey has been incredible.

The Early Days

Back in the 1990s, the internet was a new and unexplored space. Websites were mostly text-heavy, using basic **HTML** and very limited styling through **CSS**.
